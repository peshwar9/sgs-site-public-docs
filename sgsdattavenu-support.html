SGS Datta Venu - Support

Thank you for using SGS Datta Venu. If you need help or have any questions, we are here to assist you.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for live flute classes?

Go to the About tab in the app and fill in the registration form with your details. We will contact you with class details.

What do I need to start learning?

All you need is a bansuri (Indian bamboo flute) and a willingness to learn. The app provides video lessons for all levels.

How do I request deletion of my data?

Send an email to sgsdattavenu@gmail.com with your name and email address, and we will delete your data promptly.
