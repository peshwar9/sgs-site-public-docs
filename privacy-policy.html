Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

SGS Datta Venu ("the App") is a free flute learning application. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

The App collects personal information only when you voluntarily register for live flute classes. The registration form collects:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Country and city
  • Flute experience level
  • Years associated with Sri Swamiji
  • Preferred language of instruction
  • Preferred timing for online classes

How We Use Your Information

Your registration data is used solely to coordinate live flute class enrollment and communicate class details to you. We do not use your data for any other purpose.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Analytics and Tracking

The App does not use any analytics, advertising, or crash reporting tools. No usage data is collected automatically.

Data Storage and Security

Your registration data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at sgsdattavenu@gmail.com. We will process your request promptly.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at sgsdattavenu@gmail.com.
